Bio: The purpose of the site is to provide a space for musicians to work together more easily. Users would post their music and according to their preferences they can invite people to add on to their work or people can request to collab. Users would post their FLP files directly to the site. It’s important to create this site because it’s harder nowadays to find third spaces to congregate and explore hobbies together.
